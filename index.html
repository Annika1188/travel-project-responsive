Why Blue Mountains?

Blue Mountians, World Heritage National Park

The Blue Mountains National Park is a protected national park that is located in the Blue Mountains region of New South Wales, in eastern Australia. The 267,954-hectare (662,130-acre) national park is situated approximately 80 kilometres (50 mi) west of the Sydney CBD, and the park boundary is quite irregular as it is broken up by roads, urban areas and areas of private property.[3] Despite the name mountains, the area is an uplifted plateau, dissected by a number of larger rivers. The highest point in the park is Mount Werong at 1,215 metres (3,986 ft) above sea level; while the low point is on the Nepean River at 20 metres (66 ft) above sea level as it leaves the park.

Fed blue mountains is open weekdays from 6:30 am and weekends from 7.30 am serving a wide variety of fresh traditional homemade food and drinks in a warm and friendly atmosphere. Our goal is to satisfy and delight you by providing superior quality home-made foods and healthy options.Dine-in or Takeaway? We have it covered! Coffee, Juices, milkshakes, thick shakes, smoothies. Sandwiches, pies, cakes, salads, deli items, frittatas, curries, burgers, soup…and more. Our variety of food and drinks are available for dine-in or takeaway.

Their Story

Before starting ‘fed blue mountains’ in 2016, both owners were in education. They travelled long distances from the Blue Mountains for work every day. They wanted to spend more time in the picturesque Blue Mountains, not just on weekends, so they purchased Fed Café and opened the doors of ‘fed blue mountains’.

Love Bites Coffee Co

Lovebites Coffee Co is a gourmet coffee, breakfast, brunch and lunch destination in the heart of Wentworth Falls Village on a mission to deliver best in class service, and a world-class dining experience. Offering a fresh take on the classics and an exciting host of new additions, our gourmet eats are filled to the brim with the freshest ingredients available.

Address:

13 Station St, Wentworth Falls 2782

Their Story

Food providence is important to us, as is showcasing the amazing produce of the Blue Mountains and Central West. Serving locally roasted coffee and meals featuring freshly baked local bread, cheese, free-range eggs and more, we aim to cater to the growing mass of consumers who care about where their food has come from. Catering to the rising health-consciousness of consumers, a range of dairy-free, vegetarian and gluten-free menu items are also available. Lovebites lives on a philosophy of ‘fresh tasty food for all’.
